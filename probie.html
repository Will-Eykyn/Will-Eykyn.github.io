Probie Technologies

February 2025 – June 2025  ·  Tufts University Derby New Ventures Competition
🏆 3rd Place & $5,000 Award — Derby New Ventures Competition 2025

Overview

Probie Technologies was a startup concept developed to address a concrete inefficiency in law enforcement: police report writing. Through field research across multiple police departments, we identified that officers were spending up to an hour per report on manual administrative tasks — time that could be returned to the community through better tooling.

We designed a cloud-based Computer Aided Dispatch (CAD) and Records Management System featuring AI-assisted tools to automate evidence synthesis and streamline report generation. The vision was a suite of automated efficiency tools that would reduce reliance on manual data compilation and return thousands of officer-hours per year back to public service.

What We Built

  • Identified and quantified administrative inefficiencies in police report-writing workflows through field research across multiple departments, uncovering opportunities to reduce report completion time by up to 1 hour per report.
  • Designed a cloud-based system architecture with AI-assisted tools to automate evidence synthesis and streamline report generation, reducing reliance on manual data compilation.
  • Developed a full business plan and pitch, advancing through three competitive rounds in the Derby New Ventures Competition at Tufts University.
  • Secured 3rd place and a $5,000 award against a competitive field of student ventures.
